Introduction

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is revolutionizing various sectors, contributing to social good in areas like healthcare, education, and disaster response.

Applications of AI in Social Good

  • Healthcare: AI helps in disease diagnosis and personalized treatments.
  • Education: AI-powered tools enhance learning experiences.
  • Disaster Response: AI aids in predicting and mitigating disasters.
  • Environmental Protection: AI supports sustainability and conservation efforts.

Challenges and Ethical Concerns

Despite its benefits, AI raises ethical concerns like bias, privacy, and accountability that need careful consideration.

Conclusion

AI has immense potential for social good. Responsible AI development and governance will ensure its positive impact on society.
